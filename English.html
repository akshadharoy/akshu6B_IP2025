The life of

ILA SACHANI

Introduction - Who is Ila Sachani?

Ila Sachani, a talented Kathiawar embriodery artist, overcomes physical challenges to excel in her crafts.
Her skills were recognised at a state exhibition which lead to recognition and national awards.
Ila was born with a deformity that rendered her arms useless, she taught her feet the art og making embroidery.
Come let's explore the life and work of Ila Sachani.



Beginning - How It All Started...

Ila's journey began in Amreli, Gujarat, where she was born into a farmer's family.
It was hard for her to doo anything with her loose hands. She watched other children do various
things and enjoy as she wished to join them.
Ila's mother and grandmother were focused to help her overcome this challenge.



Progress - Overcoming Difficulties...

They were skilled in the traditional art of Kathiawar and decided to share this art with Ila.
As her hands were disabled, they taught her embroidery using her feet!
This was certainly not an easy task but nor impossible for Ila
as she accepted the challenge with determination.
At first sewing a needle was hard, but Ila didn't give up and became an expert at a young age.
They taught her to create beautiful patterns using her feet. Her younger brother and sister helped too.
She learned many art style outside Gujarat; Kaichhi, Kasmiri, Lucknawi, and others.
For Ila, embriodery became a way to express herself.



Opportunities - The Word Spreads...

At first, she crafted fine designs only for her family and friends, they were amazed and talked about these designs to others.
Soon the word spread in the village, it also reached the government office in Surat who
were moved by Ila's special skills. They decided to display some of her embriodery at a state exhibition.
This opened new doors for Ila as people started buying her art. Ila was now indeed famous, encouraged by this popularity,
she participated in more exhibitions, not just within sthe state level but at the national level.



Accomplishments - Embroided Dreams By Feet...

Ila recieved many awards and honours, not just for her art but also for willpower and creativity she has shown in her work.
Ila Sachani, once a farmer's daughter facing a tough challenge, has made that challenge into a victory
and now is a celebrated artist and reciever of the President's medal. The most important change for Ila was the new independence.
Throough her skill, she not only earned a regular income but also found joy in doing what she loved.
She proved that challenges can be surpassed with focus, hard work, positivity and never giving up.



Summary - The Present...

Now when you enter a tiny room in Moti Vavdi village, Bhavnagar, Gujarat, you will see many lovely things like cushions, bedcovers, pillows and more with beautiful designs all made by Ila Sachani. Ila's story reminds us that art has the power to rise above physical boundaries and touch the soul. It also teaches us that dreams are made to be achieved, challenges are made to be overcomed and talents are made to be shown.
